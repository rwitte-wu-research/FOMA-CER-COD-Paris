## Methods

One joint 3LMA-RVE model per coding: `rma.mv(zi, V, mods = ~0 + factor(coding), random = ~1|cluster_id/study/esid, REML)` with CHE working covariance (ρ = 0.6 on cluster_id [F56]) and CR2/Satterthwaite inference clustered on cluster_id; cell means plus the CR2 linear contrast (post − pre) as the moderation test with its own formal inference [DEC-031a.6]. Headline coding `pp_mid_lag0` (post iff post-2015 window share ≥ 0.5, ties→Post [DEC-024]); sensitivities: tie-break (ties→Pre), any-exposure end-coding (lags 0–3, lags 1–3 appendix), share recuts at 2017/18/19 (computed in-script per P2), clean-window contrast (pre-only 611 ES/27 studies vs post-only 318/13; full inference, upgrade fired [DEC-031a.5]). Semantics pins P1–P5 all asserted on the data (P1: `pp_share_lag0 ≡ share_2016`, 41 distinct values; P3: recut reproduces the headline exactly; P4: 79 tie disagreements, all at 0.5). LOSO: 31 whole-study drops over the post cell. No pairwise spec-vs-headline tests; joint specification inference is N5's job (Block H).

## Results

**Paris moderation (B1).** diff_z = **+0.010**, 95% CI [−0.028; +0.049], t(15.0) = 0.56, **p = 0.582** (cells: pre r = −0.062 [−0.097; −0.026], 1,994 ES/83 studies; post r = −0.051 [−0.073; −0.030], 711/31). A precise null: the CI excludes any shift larger than ~80% of the pooled effect itself, in either direction. Realized contrast df 15.0 vs design-only df 31.9 — the design quantity omits estimated variance components; both clear the Tipton floor; disclosed. Cell means differ slightly from T1/A3 by construction (joint model with shared σ² vs separate subset fits).

**Coding sensitivity (B3).** No collapse anywhere: tie-break +0.010 (p = .59); share recuts 2017/18/19 = +0.008/+0.003/+0.004 (p ≥ .66); end-family −0.019/−0.006/+0.040/+0.021 (all p ≥ .14, lags 1–3 appendix); clean-window **−0.013** [−0.049; +0.023] (p = .46, df 23.5). Sign heterogeneity around zero — full-domain codings lean weakly positive, the clean contrast weakly negative — is itself evidence against any systematic shift. The end-family's instability is explained, not mysterious: it measures early partial exposure (see B7).

**Post-cell leverage (B6).** All 31 LOSO diffs ∈ [+0.002; +0.014]; range 0.011 (< ⅓ of the CI half-width); extremes: drop Li et al (2022) → +0.002 (df 51.9), drop Brinette et al (2026) → +0.014; nothing approaches significance. Dominance: Panjwani et al (2023) = 22.8% weight / 23.9% ES share (170/711 — memo confirmed), top-3 ≈ 44%; concentrated but demonstrably immaterial.

**Disclosures (B7, v12-canonical).** Attenuation: **66.0%** of end-coded "post" ES are majority-pre (1,383/2,094; 56 studies; down from ~81% pre-v12) — disqualifies end-coding as headline. Knife-edge: **2.9%** of ES within 0.05 of the cut (79; 5 studies; identical to the tie set) — the headline cut is not threshold-fragile. Panel drift: **+0.015** (ES-level mean exposure 0.358 vs study-level 0.343) — disclosed, immaterial; formal time-composition handling is T8's.
